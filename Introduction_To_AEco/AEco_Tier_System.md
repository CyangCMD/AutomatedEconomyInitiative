# AEco Tier System

From: Introduction_To_AEco

Date: March 26, 2026

Author: Cyang

---------------------

This is the walkthrough of the AEco Skeleton's Tier system. All contents are modularized and follow an assessment protocol to ensure submissions are aligned with it. Below are the tiers for the AEco Skeleton and what they represent:

- Tier 1

This is the founding layer of the skeleton. Everything that following layers would be built would draw axioms from their respective areas. Think of it as the "skeleton" that binds each sector of the skeleton into a coherent unit. Here, economics, governance and whatnot share the same baseline that define what their paths would be guided by.

- Tier 2

The abstraction that is built on top of Tier 1. Concepts and models are defined with Tier 1 axiomatics. It represents the bare modulars that begin to take form.

- Tier 3

Paths from the founding layer begin to diverge from here. Abstractions from Level 2 take on shape and solidify with complex models that explain them with greater detail. Simulations on economic policy, political constructions and societal implications can be made since the concrete would be more visible. 

- Tier 4

The final tier of the skeleton. Each path matures on this level and can afford to be experimental. Downstream effects on culture, policies, economic conditions and whatnot are observed here. There is room to make hypotheses and experiment with custom additions.
