# AEco Github Walkthrough

From: Introduction to AEco
Date: March 26, 2026
Author: Cyang


This is the walkthrough of the AEco Github. All contents are modularized and follow a strict
assessment protocol to ensure submissions are within respective vectors. Below are the modulars for AEco and what they represent:

- Level 1

This is the founding layer for AEco. Everything that following layers would be built would draw axioms from their respective areas. Think of it as the "skeleton" that binds each sector of AEco into a coherent unit. Here, economics, governance and whatnot share the same baseline that define what their paths would be guided by.

- Level 2

The abstraction that is built on top of Level 1. Concepts and models are defined with Level 1 axiomatics. It represents the bare modulars that begin to take form.

- Level 3

Paths from the founding layer begin to diverge from here. Abstractions from Level 2 take on shape and solidify with complex models that explain them with greater detail. Simulations on economic policy, political constructions and societal implications can be made since the concrete would be more visible. 

- Level 4

The final level of AEco. Each path matures on this level and can afford to be experimental. Downstream effects on culture, policies, economic conditions and whatnot are observed here. There is room to make hypotheses and experiment with custom additions.
